About me

Who am I? A 27 year old Front-end developing student at Medieinstitutet.
After high school I didn't picture myself being in the web dev field and worked a few different jobs.
One day I decided to try something different, I had taken a web development course in high school but that was really it.
Now here we are and I'm so glad that I made the decision to jump into the unknown world of Front-end.

I am an avid fan of martial arts and have been for the better part of my life.
When I was around 6 I began training judo with my father, my judo career lasted around 5-6 years.
At the ripe age of 17 me and my friend visited a thaiboxing gym called Svea Muay Thai in Södermalm, Stockholm where we began training.
Even though we no longer reside at that particular gym, we still train muay thai to this very day, around 10 years later!
When I am not sitting behind my laptop or sweating in the gym I enjoy skiing, music of all sorts, the sun and my friends and family!
